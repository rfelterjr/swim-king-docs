## Long description (~4000 chars max App Store, longer on Play)

Swim King is a one-tap, no-nonsense arcade game starring a tiny sea turtle on a one-way trip into the dark.

Tap to flap. Don't hit the coral. Don't sink. Don't stop.

The water is calm at first. Then the coral starts closing in. Then it closes some more. Most players cap out under 30 meters. Reaching 100m makes you a legend. There is no end — only your own line in the sand, growing further away.

**What's in it:**
- One mechanic, one finger, fifteen seconds to learn
- Endless arcade — your high score is your only enemy
- Tightening difficulty curve calibrated to be brutal past 50m
- Near-miss bonus rewards tight flying (the closer you graze, the more you score)
- Milestone callouts at 25m, 50m, and 100m for the deep divers
- Dark, calming underwater aesthetic — no clutter, no noise

Made for the cracks in your day. One run takes a minute. One more run takes another minute. Suddenly it's been an hour.

Swim deeper.
